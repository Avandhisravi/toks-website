JOURNEY

The evolutionary path of a global vision.

2018

Founded by Syino C. Mathew, Albin C. Mathew, and Ashly C. Mathew, the company began its journey with a focus on mobile video editing and content creation through YouTube.

2020

Toks Enterprise was established, marking the company’s strategic shift toward marketing services.

2022

Expanded operations to include domestic export services with the launch of Toks Export.

2024–2025

Entered a growth phase with the launch of multiple divisions, including Toks Foundation and Toks Finance.

2025

Announced the launch of SureSlot, a dedicated study abroad division.

2025 DECEMBER

Opened the company’s first physical office in Kattappana, supported by a team of 50+ in-house staff and 80+ remote professionals.

JUNE 4:UK OFFICE

Established a virtual presence in the United Kingdom, later expanding with a commission-based team.

JANUARY 19: SINGAPORE

Officially launched the Singapore office, marking international expansion.

MARCH 2: JERUSALEM

Expanded global footprint with the opening of the Jerusalem office.

UPCOMING: DUBAI

Preparations underway for the launch of our Dubai office, expanding our presence in the Middle East.
